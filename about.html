About Us

Our Products are made from 100% natural bamboo, ensuring durability and sustainability.

Company Information

DDH-Superior is a company specializing in the research, development, manufacturing, and sales of bamboo, wood, and hardware furniture and home products. The factory is located in Fengxin, Jiangxi, covering 25,000 square meters, and owns over 10,000 acres of bamboo forest with over 15 years of manufacturing experience.

Our Factory

The factory is located in Fengxin, Jiangxi, covering 25,000 square meters, and owns over 10,000 acres of bamboo forest with over 15 years of manufacturing experience.
